Who We Are

Pro Timber is a Pretoria-based joinery and shopfitting company with an experienced management team and expert artisans. We work with commercial, residential, and corporate clients to deliver high-quality, custom-crafted timber solutions.

Every project is built to last — from bespoke cabinetry and built-in furniture to full shopfitting installations for retail and office environments.

What We Do

Shopfitting

Custom retail and office interiors built to your exact specifications.

Joinery

Doors, frames, cabinetry, and built-in furniture crafted by skilled artisans.

Residential
